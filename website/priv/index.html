flyradar

Manage Your Fly.io Resources In Style With flyradar

flyradar is basically k9s for the platform Fly.io

It continually watches for changes and lets you manage and observe the associated fly.io resources.

  • Navigate around your existing apps, machines, volumes and secrets.
  • Open, restart and destroy your apps, view releases and services of them.
  • Start, restart, stop, suspend, kill, destroy, cordon, uncordon your VMs.
  • Monitor, filter and dump the logs of your apps and VMs.
  • Destroy your volumes, stage unset your secrets.

Terminal

Installation

Note

flyradar is an OSS third-party tool and is not an official Fly.io project.

Prerequisites

flyradar relies on the Fly CLI for authentication and its built-in agent for operational functionality. Make sure you have flyctl installed on your system before proceeding.

Cargo
cargo install flyradar
Homebrew
brew install furkankly/tap/flyradar
Binaries

Binaries for Linux, Windows and Mac are available as tarballs in the release page.
